Euclidean Algorithm

Finding the Greatest Common Divisor (GCD)
For natural numbers a and b, when dividing a by b and the remainder is r:
a = bq + r
The greatest common divisor of a and b is equal to the greatest common divisor of b and r.
gcd(a, b) → gcd(b, r) equal
By repeating this process, you can find the GCD of two integers.
This is called the Euclidean Algorithm.
