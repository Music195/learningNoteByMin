Condition for a Quadrilateral to be Cyclic

Circle Geometry
A quadrilateral is cyclic if either of the following holds:
[1] The sum of one pair of opposite angles is 180°.
[2] An interior angle is equal to the exterior angle of its opposite angle.
If one pair of opposite angles adds to 180°,
or an angle equals the exterior angle of its opposite,
the quadrilateral is cyclic.
