Platonic Solids

Regular Polyhedra in Geometry
A convex polyhedron that satisfies the following conditions is called a regular polyhedron (Platonic solid):
  1. All faces are congruent regular polygons.
  2. The number of faces meeting at each vertex is the same for all vertices.
