Triangle Side Length Inequality

Triangle Geometry
  1. In any triangle:
    [1] The sum of the lengths of any two sides is greater than the length of the third side.
  2. [2] The difference of the lengths of any two sides is less than the length of the third side.
Triangle with sides a, b, c
ⓘ For a triangle with side lengths a, b, c, the triangle exists if:
\( |b - c| < a < b + c \)
