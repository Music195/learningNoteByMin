Correlation Coefficient (相関係数)

Data Analysis
Let the covariance of \( x \) and \( y \) be \( s_{xy} \), and the standard deviations of \( x \) and \( y \) be \( s_x \) and \( s_y \), respectively.
The correlation coefficient \( r \) is defined as:
\( r = \dfrac{s_{xy}}{s_x s_y} \)
💡 The value of \( r \) approaches 1 as the positive correlation becomes stronger, and approaches -1 as the negative correlation becomes stronger.
