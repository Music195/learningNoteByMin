Quartiles (四分位数)

Data Analysis
When data is arranged in order, the values that divide the data into four equal parts are called quartiles.
From smallest to largest, these are called
first quartile, second quartile, third quartile, denoted as \( Q_1, Q_2, Q_3 \).
When data size is 9
2 6 8 9 13 16 19 21 29
↑
Q₁=9
↑
Q₂=16
↑
Q₃=21
When data size is 8
2 6 8 9 13 16 19 21
↑
Q₁=9
↑
Q₂=14.5
↑
Q₃=19
💡 Using quartiles allows you to compare the degree of spread (dispersion) of data, which cannot be understood by representative values alone.
