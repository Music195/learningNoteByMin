Positive & Negative Correlation (正の相関・負の相関)

Data Analysis
In data consisting of two variables:

Positive correlation (正の相関):
When one variable increases and the other also tends to increase.

Negative correlation (負の相関):
When one variable increases and the other tends to decrease.

💡 Correlation describes the relationship between two variables. Positive correlation means both increase together, negative correlation means one increases as the other decreases.
