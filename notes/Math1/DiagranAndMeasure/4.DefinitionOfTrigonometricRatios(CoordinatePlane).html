Definition of Trigonometric Ratios (Coordinate Plane)
Let $\angle POQ = \theta$. Place point $Q$ on the semicircle of radius $r$, and let the coordinates of $Q$ be $(x, y)$.
$\displaystyle \sin\theta = \frac{\color{#e65100}{y}}{r}$
$\displaystyle \cos\theta = \frac{\color{#e65100}{x}}{r}$
$\displaystyle \tan\theta = \frac{\color{#e65100}{y}}{x}$
