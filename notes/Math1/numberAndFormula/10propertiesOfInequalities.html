Properties of Inequalities
1 $A < B$ then $A + C < B + C$
2 $A < B$ then $A - C < B - C$
3 $A < B$, $C > 0$ then $AC < BC$, $\dfrac{A}{C} < \dfrac{B}{C}$
4 $A < B$, $C < 0$ then $AC > BC$, $\dfrac{A}{C} > \dfrac{B}{C}$
💡
For inequalities, if you multiply or divide both sides by a negative number, the direction of the inequality sign reverses.
