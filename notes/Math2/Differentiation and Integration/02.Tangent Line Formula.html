Tangent Line Equation at a Point on the Graph
The equation of the tangent line at the point \( (a,\, f(a)) \) on the graph of \( y = f(x) \) is:
\( y - f(a) = f'(a)(x - a) \)
The slope of the tangent line at the point \( (a,\, f(a)) \) on the graph of \( y = f(x) \) is equal to the derivative \( f'(a) \) at \( x = a \).
