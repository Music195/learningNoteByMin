Definite Integral and Area (Case 2)
For \( a \leq x \leq b \) and \( f(x) \leq 0 \), the area \( S \) bounded by the graph of \( y = f(x) \), the x-axis, and the lines \( x = a \), \( x = b \) is:
\( S = \int_a^b \color{#f57c00}{-f(x)}\,dx \)
When \( f(x) \leq 0 \), consider the area using the graph of \( y = -f(x) \), which is symmetric to \( y = f(x) \) with respect to the x-axis.
