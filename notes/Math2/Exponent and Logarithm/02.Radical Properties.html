Properties of Radicals
For \( a > 0,\, b > 0 \), and positive integers \( m,\, n,\, p \):
1. \( \sqrt[n]{a} \sqrt[n]{b} = \sqrt[n]{ab} \)
2. \( \sqrt[n]{\dfrac{a}{b}} = \dfrac{\sqrt[n]{a}}{\sqrt[n]{b}} \)
3. \( (\sqrt[n]{a})^m = \sqrt[n]{a^m} \)
4. \( \sqrt[m]{\sqrt[n]{a}} = \sqrt[mn]{a} \)
5. \( \sqrt[n]{a^m} = (\sqrt[n]{a})^m \)
