Graph of Logarithmic Functions
The graph of \( y = \log_a x \) passes through the points \( (1, 0) \) and \( (a, 1) \), and has the y-axis as a vertical asymptote.
It is symmetric to the graph of \( y = a^x \) with respect to the line \( y = x \).
When \( a > 1 \): Increasing curve
When \( 0 < a < 1 \): Decreasing curve
The graph of \( y = \log_a x \) is symmetric to the graph of \( y = a^x \) with respect to the line \( y = x \).
