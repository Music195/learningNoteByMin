Polynomial Division

Division Algorithm for Polynomials
When dividing a polynomial \(A\) by a polynomial \(B\), the quotient is \(Q\) and the remainder is \(R\):
\( \text{Dividend} = \text{Divisor} \times \text{Quotient} + \text{Remainder} \)
The remainder \(R\) is either 0 or a polynomial of lower degree than \(B\).
