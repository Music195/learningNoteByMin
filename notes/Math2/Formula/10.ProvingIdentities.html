Proving Identities \( A = B \)
To prove the identity \( A = B \), the following methods can be used:
  1. Transform one side (\( A \) or \( B \)) to derive the other.
  2. Transform both \( A \) and \( B \) to derive the same expression.
  3. Transform \( A - B \) to show that it equals 0.
Proving identities often involves algebraic manipulation to simplify or transform expressions. Ensure all steps are logically valid.
