Arithmetic Mean - Geometric Mean Inequality
Given \( a > 0 \) and \( b > 0 \):

\( \frac{a + b}{2} \geq \sqrt{ab} \)

Equality holds when \( a = b \).
The AM-GM inequality is often used in the form \( a > 0, b > 0 \) and \( \frac{a + b}{2} \geq \sqrt{ab} \). It is a fundamental inequality in mathematics.
