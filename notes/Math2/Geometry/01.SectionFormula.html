Section Formula
For two points \( A(a) \) and \( B(b) \) on a number line:

The coordinate of the internal division point \( P \) dividing \( AB \) in the ratio \( m:n \) is:
\( P = \frac{ma + nb}{m + n} \)

The coordinate of the external division point \( Q \) dividing \( AB \) in the ratio \( m:n \) is:
\( Q = \frac{-ma + nb}{m - n} \)

The midpoint of \( AB \) is:
\( \frac{a + b}{2} \)
The internal division formula can be converted to the external division formula by replacing \( n \) with \( -n \).
