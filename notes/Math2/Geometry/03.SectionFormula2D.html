Section Formula in 2D
For two points \( A(x_1, y_1) \) and \( B(x_2, y_2) \) on a plane:

The coordinates of the internal division point \( P \) dividing \( AB \) in the ratio \( m:n \) are:
\( P = \left( \frac{nx_1 + mx_2}{m + n}, \frac{ny_1 + my_2}{m + n} \right) \)

The coordinates of the external division point \( Q \) dividing \( AB \) in the ratio \( m:n \) are:
\( Q = \left( \frac{-nx_1 + mx_2}{m - n}, \frac{-ny_1 + my_2}{m - n} \right) \)

The midpoint of \( AB \) is:
\( \left( \frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2} \right) \)
The internal division formula can be converted to the external division formula by replacing \( n \) with \( -n \).
