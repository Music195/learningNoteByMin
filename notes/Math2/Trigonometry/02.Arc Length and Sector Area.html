Arc Length and Sector Area
For a circle with radius \( r \) and a central angle \( \theta \) (in radians):

- The arc length \( l \) is given by:
$$l = r\theta$$ - The area \( S \) of the sector is given by:
$$S = \frac{1}{2}r^2\theta \quad \text{or} \quad S = \frac{1}{2}lr$$
The arc length and sector area are directly proportional to the central angle \( \theta \) when measured in radians.
