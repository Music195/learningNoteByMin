Trigonometric Properties
Below are the periodicity and symmetry properties of trigonometric functions:
\( \sin(\theta + 2n\pi) = \sin\theta \)
\( \cos(\theta + 2n\pi) = \cos\theta \quad \text{where } n \text{ is an integer} \)
\( \tan(\theta + 2n\pi) = \tan\theta \)
\( \sin(-\theta) = -\sin\theta \)
\( \cos(-\theta) = \cos\theta \)
\( \tan(-\theta) = -\tan\theta \)
These equations represent the periodicity and symmetry properties of trigonometric functions.
