Half-Angle and Double-Angle Trigonometric Formulas
1. \( \sin^2 \frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{1 - \cos\alpha}{2} \)
2. \( \cos^2 \frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{1 + \cos\alpha}{2} \)
3. \( \tan^2 \frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{1 - \cos\alpha}{1 + \cos\alpha} \)
These are the half-angle formulas for sine, cosine, and tangent.
1. \( \sin 2\alpha = 2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha \)
2. \( \cos 2\alpha = \cos^2\alpha - \sin^2\alpha \)
     \( = 1 - 2\sin^2\alpha \)
     \( = 2\cos^2\alpha - 1 \)
3. \( \tan 2\alpha = \frac{2\tan\alpha}{1 - \tan^2\alpha} \)
These are the double-angle formulas for sine, cosine, and tangent.
