Definition of the Derivative
For a function $f(x)$, the derivative at $x = a$ is defined as:
$f'(a) = \displaystyle\lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(a+h) - f(a)}{h} = \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(x) - f(a)}{x - a}$
If $f(x)$ is differentiable at $x = a$, the derivative $f'(a)$ represents the slope of the tangent line to the curve $y = f(x)$ at the point $A(a, f(a))$.
