Parametric Derivatives
When $x = f(t)$, $y = g(t)$:

$\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{\color{#e65100}{\dfrac{dy}{dt}}}{\color{#e65100}{\dfrac{dx}{dt}}} = \color{#e65100}{\frac{g'(t)}{f'(t)}} $
For parametric curves, the derivative $\frac{dy}{dx}$ is found by dividing $\frac{dy}{dt}$ by $\frac{dx}{dt}$.
