Definite Integrals of Even and Odd Functions

Integration
1 For even function \( f(x) \):
\( \displaystyle \int_{-a}^{a} f(x)\, dx = 2 \int_{0}^{a} f(x)\, dx \)
2 For odd function \( f(x) \):
\( \displaystyle \int_{-a}^{a} f(x)\, dx = 0 \)
Note: If \( f(-x) = f(x) \) always holds, the function is called even.
If \( f(-x) = -f(x) \) always holds, the function is called odd.
