Intersection and Union of Sets

Visualizing Intersection and Union in Set Theory
The set of all elements that belong to A and B is called the intersection of A and B.
This is written as A ∩ B.
The set of all elements that belong to A or B (or both) is called the union of A and B.
This is written as A ∪ B.
