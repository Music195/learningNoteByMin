Divisibility Rules

How to Check for Multiples

Divisibility Rules 1

  • For multiples of 2 … the unit digit is one of 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8
  • For multiples of 4 … the last two digits form a multiple of 4
  • For multiples of 5 … the unit digit is 0 or 5
  • For multiples of 8 … the last three digits form a multiple of 8
  • For multiples of 10 … the unit digit is 0

Divisibility Rules 2

  • For multiples of 3 … the sum of the digits is a multiple of 3
  • For multiples of 9 … the sum of the digits is a multiple of 9
