Counting the Number of Divisors of a Natural Number
If the prime factorization of a natural number N is
N = paqbrc···,
then the number of positive divisors of N is:
(a + 1)(b + 1)(c + 1)···
Example: For 23 · 32:
23 has divisors: 20, 21, 22, 23 (3+1 divisors)
32 has divisors: 30, 31, 32 (2+1 divisors)
The divisors of 23 · 32 are all products of a divisor of 23 and a divisor of 32.
So, the number of divisors of 23 · 32 is (3+1) × (2+1) = 12.
