Sum of Arithmetic Sequence
Let \( S_n \) be the sum of the first \( n \) terms of an arithmetic sequence.
1. When the first term is \( a \), last term is \( l \):
\( S_n = \frac{1}{2} n \color{#f57c00}{(a + l)} \)
2. When the first term is \( a \), common difference is \( d \):
\( S_n = \frac{1}{2} n \color{#f57c00}{\{2a + (n-1)d\}} \)
In a finite sequence, the number of terms is called the 項数 (number of terms), and the last term is called the 末項 (last term).
The first formula expresses the sum of an arithmetic sequence with first term \( a \), last term \( l \), and \( n \) terms.
