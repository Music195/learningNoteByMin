Sum Formulas for Natural Numbers
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n c = \color{#f57c00}{nc} \qquad \text{especially} \qquad \sum_{k=1}^n 1 = \color{#f57c00}{n} \)
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n k = \color{#f57c00}{\frac{1}{2} n(n+1)} \)
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n k^2 = \color{#f57c00}{\frac{1}{6} n(n+1)(2n+1)} \)
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n k^3 = \color{#f57c00}{\left\{ \frac{1}{2} n(n+1) \right\}^2} \)
