Properties of Sigma (Σ)
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n (a_k + b_k) = \) \( \sum_{k=1}^n a_k + \sum_{k=1}^n b_k \)
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n p a_k = \) \( p \sum_{k=1}^n a_k \)   where \( p \) is a constant independent of \( k \)
\( \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n (a_k - b_k) = \color{#1976d2}{\sum_{k=1}^n a_k - \sum_{k=1}^n b_k} \)   also holds.
