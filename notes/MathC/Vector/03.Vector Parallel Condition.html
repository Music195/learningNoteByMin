Condition for Parallel Vectors
When \( \vec{a} \neq \vec{0},\ \vec{b} \neq \vec{0} \)
\( \vec{a} \parallel \vec{b} \iff \) \( \vec{b} = k\vec{a} \) for some real number \( k \)
In \( \vec{b} = k\vec{a} \), if \( k > 0 \), \( \vec{b} \) is parallel to \( \vec{a} \) in the same direction.
If \( k < 0 \), \( \vec{b} \) is parallel to \( \vec{a} \) in the opposite direction.
