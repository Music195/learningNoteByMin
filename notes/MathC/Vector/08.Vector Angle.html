Angle Between Two Vectors
Assuming \( \vec{a} = (a_1, a_2) \) and \( \vec{b} = (b_1, b_2) \) are non-zero vectors, and \( \theta \) is the angle between them:

$$ \cos\theta = \frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{|\vec{a}| |\vec{b}|} = \frac{a_1b_1 + a_2b_2}{\sqrt{a_1^2 + a_2^2} \sqrt{b_1^2 + b_2^2}} $$
where \( 0^\circ \le \theta \le 180^\circ \).
