Points on a Straight Line
When 2 points A and B are distinct, point C lies on the line AB
$$ \iff \text{there exists a real number } \color{#f57c00}k \color{black}\text{ such that } \overrightarrow{\text{AC}} = \color{#f57c00}k \color{black}\overrightarrow{\text{AB}} $$
