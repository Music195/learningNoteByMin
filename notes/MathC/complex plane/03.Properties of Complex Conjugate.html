3. Properties of Complex Conjugate
1 \(\overline{\alpha + \beta} = \overline{\alpha} + \overline{\beta}\)
2 \(\overline{\alpha - \beta} = \overline{\alpha} - \overline{\beta}\)
3 \(\overline{\alpha \beta} = \overline{\alpha} \, \overline{\beta}\)
4 \(\overline{\left(\dfrac{\alpha}{\beta}\right)} = \dfrac{\overline{\alpha}}{\overline{\beta}}\)
Note:
  • For any natural number \( n \), \( \overline{\alpha^n} = (\overline{\alpha})^n \).
