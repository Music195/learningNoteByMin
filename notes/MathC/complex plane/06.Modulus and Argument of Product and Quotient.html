6. Modulus and Argument of Product and Quotient
1 \( |\alpha\beta| = |\alpha|\,|\beta| \qquad \arg(\alpha\beta) = \arg\alpha + \arg\beta \)
2 \( \left|\frac{\alpha}{\beta}\right| = \frac{|\alpha|}{|\beta|} \qquad \arg\left(\frac{\alpha}{\beta}\right) = \arg\alpha - \arg\beta \)
Note:
  • For arguments, differences of integer multiples of \(2\pi\) are ignored.
