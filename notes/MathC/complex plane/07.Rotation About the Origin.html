7. Rotation About the Origin

For \( \alpha = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta \) and any complex number \( z \), the point \( \alpha z \) is the point obtained by rotating \( z \) about the origin by angle \( \theta \).

Note:
  • Multiplying by \( \cos\theta + i\sin\theta \) rotates a point by \( \theta \) counterclockwise about the origin.
