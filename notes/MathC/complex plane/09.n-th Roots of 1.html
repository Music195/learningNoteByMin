n-th Roots of 1

The n-th roots of 1 are the following \( n \) complex numbers:

$$ z_k = \cos\left(\frac{2k\pi}{n}\right) + i\sin\left(\frac{2k\pi}{n}\right) $$
\( (k = 0,\ 1,\ 2,\ \ldots,\ n-1) \)
Note:
  • For \( n \geq 3 \), the points representing the n-th roots of unity are the vertices of a regular n-gon inscribed in the unit circle.
  • In particular, one of the vertices is at the point \( 1 \).
