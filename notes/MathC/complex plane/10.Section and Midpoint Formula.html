10. Section and Midpoint Formula

For two points \( A(\alpha) \), \( B(\beta) \), the point dividing the segment \( AB \) in the ratio \( m:n \) is:

Internal division: $$ \frac{n\alpha + m\beta}{m+n} $$
External division: $$ \frac{-n\alpha + m\beta}{m-n} $$
Midpoint of \( AB \): $$ \frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} $$
