1. Parabola
Parabola: \( y^2 = 4px \quad (p \neq 0) \)
  • Vertex: Origin O
  • Focus: Point \( (p,\,0) \)
  • Directrix: Line \( x = -p \)
  • Axis: \( x \)-axis, symmetric with respect to the \( x \)-axis
For any point on the parabola, the distance to the focus equals the distance to the directrix.
