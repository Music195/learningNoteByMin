2. Ellipse
Ellipse: \( \dfrac{x^2}{a^2} + \dfrac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \quad (a > b > 0) \)
  • Center: Origin O
  • Foci: \( \left( \pm\sqrt{a^2-b^2},\,0 \right) \)
  • Major axis length: \( 2a \)
  • Minor axis length: \( 2b \)
For any point on the ellipse, the sum of the distances to the two foci is constant (\(2a\)).
