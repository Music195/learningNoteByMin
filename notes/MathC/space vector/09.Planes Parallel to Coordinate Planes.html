9. Equations of Planes Parallel to Coordinate Planes

Plane Parallel to the \(yz\)-Plane

A plane passing through \(A(a,\,0,\,0)\) and parallel to the \(yz\)-plane has the equation:

$$ x = a $$

Plane Parallel to the \(zx\)-Plane

A plane passing through \(B(0,\,b,\,0)\) and parallel to the \(zx\)-plane has the equation:

$$ y = b $$

Plane Parallel to the \(xy\)-Plane

A plane passing through \(C(0,\,0,\,c)\) and parallel to the \(xy\)-plane has the equation:

$$ z = c $$
Note:
  • The plane \(x = a\) is perpendicular to the \(x\)-axis.
  • The plane \(y = b\) is perpendicular to the \(y\)-axis.
  • The plane \(z = c\) is perpendicular to the \(z\)-axis.
