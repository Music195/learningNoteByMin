10. Equation of a Sphere

Sphere with Center \((a,\,b,\,c)\) and Radius \(r\)

The equation of a sphere with center \((a,\,b,\,c)\) and radius \(r\) is:

$$ (x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 + (z - c)^2 = r^2 $$

Sphere with Center at the Origin

The equation of a sphere with center at the origin and radius \(r\) is:

$$ x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = r^2 $$
Note:
  • The equation represents all points \((x, y, z)\) at a distance \(r\) from the center \((a, b, c)\).
  • For the origin as center, the formula simplifies as shown above.
  • This is the standard form for the equation of a sphere in 3D space.
